About

I'm a PhD candidate at the Australian National University in Human-Computer Interaction/Human-Robot Interaction. I am passionate about the human-centred design of robots and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on designing embodied interfaces for supporting collaboration and communication in human-robot teams with extended reality technologies (virtual reality, augmented reality and mixed reality).
